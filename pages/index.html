Process

How Does VisaNation Work?

At VisaNation, we understand the importance of family unity. Our dedicated team is committed to simplifying the immigration process, ensuring you can reunite with your loved ones in the United States with ease.

Book Consultation

Step 1

Fill out the Online Questionnaire

Taking approximately 60 minutes to complete, both the sponsor and the immigrant are required to provide information about their backgrounds, family, relationship and employment . This thorough data collection enables us to customize our strategy specifically to your case.

Book Consultation

Step 2

Upload your documents

Depending on your background and relationship, we customize the documents needed for your case, ensuring a precise application package. The customization is crucial for gathering essential paperwork such as passports, birth certificates, marriage certificates, and more.

Start process

Step 3

Attorney Consultation & Review

Once we have received everything from your end, our team conducts a thorough legal review of everything submitted. We prepare a detailed package aimed at resolving any potential issues, significantly reducing the risk of relays or rejections for your filing.

Start process

Step 4

Submit package to USCIS

We compile and submit your complete package to the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), ensuring that all regulatory requirements are met for efficient processing.

Start process

Step 5

Attorney Prepration

Our attorneys will prepare for your green card interview by ensuring all is required documentation is organized and accessible. We will go over common interview questions and practice clear, confident responses to ensure you are fully prepared.
